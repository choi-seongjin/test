Note · Pre-prints & under review

Working papers live separately.

Pre-prints, under-review manuscripts, and technical reports are listed on the lab's arXiv profile and on individual authors' pages — to keep the venue of record honest. Notable current preprints include the e-scooter speed governance study (arXiv 2604.26236), the coauthorship semantic structure analysis (arXiv 2604.23699), and Weaver (arXiv 2511.08888).
